BarraCUDA by mredmond

BarraCUDA (Beginner's Analytic Research and Rendering Architecture) will be a small-scale homebrew supercomputer designed around the NVIDIA CUDA architecture. The project is meant to serve as a platform for researchers to develop parallel-programming skills, as well as deploy really cool modelling programs.
